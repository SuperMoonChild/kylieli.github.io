### Our Measurement Approach: Validating Predictive Usage

We are building a highly precise **PQL score** by stacking different usage metrics. Our method focuses on impactful activity, not just general logins.

1.  **Defining the Core Predictive Metric:** We are identifying our company's equivalent to the **"2,000 Messages"** milestoneâ€”the combination of actions that yields a reliable retention rate of $\ge 90\%$.
    * **Metrics to Stack:** This score is built from **Breadth of Usage** (number of features used), **Depth of Usage** (frequency of using core features), and confirmed engagement with **"Aha! Features."**

2.  **Identifying "Sticky" Features (Retention Drivers):**
    * **Definition:** These features provide **recurring impact**, embedding the product into the user's daily workflow.
    * **Measurement:** Using **Cohort Analysis**, we compare the 90-day retention rate of users who used Feature X versus those who did not. A significantly higher rate confirms a feature as a strong **Retention Driver**. [Imagine a chart comparing retention curves for different user cohorts.]

3.  **Identifying "Monetization" Features (Conversion Drivers):**
    * **Definition:** These features signify that the customer has reached the "limit of generosity" and is ready to upgrade, often by hitting a usage limit or needing a premium feature.
    * **Measurement:** We track the frequency and time-to-limit of features that are exclusive to the paid tier. High correlation with PQL status confirms a feature as a **Monetization Driver.**
